Email Body Analysis

## 1️⃣ Greeting
- "Dear Customer"
- Generic greeting instead of real name.
- **Suspicious:** Phishers often use generic greetings.

## 2️⃣ Opening line
- "We noticed unusual activity on your Microsoft account."
- Creates fear and urgency.
- **Suspicious:** Psychological manipulation.

## 3️⃣ Instruction
- "Click the link below to verify your account immediately."
- Directs user to click a link.
- **Suspicious:** Legit companies rarely ask this way.

## 4️⃣ Link
- http://m1crosoft-security.com/verify
- Domain mismatch; may hide real URL behind text.
- **Suspicious:** Leads to fake site.

## 5️⃣ Threat
- "Failure to verify within 24 hours will result in account suspension."
- Adds urgency/threat.
- **Suspicious:** Common phishing tactic.

## 6️⃣ Grammar/formatting issues
- Spelling mistakes or awkward phrases.
- **Suspicious:** Professional emails usually error-free.

## 7️⃣ Footer
- "© 2025 Microsoft Corporation. All rights reserved."
- May look official.
- **Check:** Verify domain info matches real company.

### Body Summary (Phishing Indicators)

| Indicator | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Generic greeting | Not personalized |
| Urgent/fear-based language | Psychological manipulation |
| Suspicious links | Domain mismatch/fake site |
| Threats | Pressure to act quickly |
| Grammar/formatting errors | Low professionalism |
| Copied footer | Tries to appear official |
